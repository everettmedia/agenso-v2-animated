# Agenso — Agent Deliverables Map

Comprehensive list of every output each agent should be able to produce. Scoped for experienced marketers who need execution speed.

---

## 1. Meta Ads Agent

### Strategy & Planning
- Campaign strategy brief (objective, audience, budget allocation, funnel stage mapping)
- Audience targeting recommendations (interest stacks, lookalike strategies, exclusion lists)
- Budget allocation plan (campaign-level pacing, CBO vs ABO recommendations)
- Funnel mapping (TOFU/MOFU/BOFU campaign architecture)
- Testing framework (creative testing plan, audience testing matrix, variable isolation)
- Scaling plan (horizontal vs vertical scaling strategy with trigger thresholds)

### Research & Analysis
- Competitive analysis (competitor ad library teardown with creative patterns, hooks, CTAs)
- Ad account audit (structure, spend efficiency, audience overlap, creative fatigue, attribution issues)
- Performance analysis report (campaign/ad set/ad level breakdown with actionable insights)
- Creative fatigue analysis (frequency trends, CTR decay, CPM spikes per creative)
- Audience overlap report (overlap percentages with consolidation recommendations)
- Attribution analysis (click vs view attribution windows, conversion path review)
- Industry benchmark comparison

### Creative — Static Ads
- Single image ad copy (primary text, headline, description — multiple variants)
- Carousel ad copy (per-card headlines + primary text)
- Collection ad copy
- Creative brief for designer (visual direction, reference examples, specs, messaging hierarchy)
- Ad copy variations matrix (hook x body x CTA combinations)
- UGC brief for creators (talking points, tone, format, dos and don'ts)

### Creative — Video Ads
- Video ad script — Reels/Stories (vertical, 15-30s, hook-first structure)
- Video ad script — Feed (square/landscape, 30-60s)
- Video ad script — UGC style (authentic, testimonial-driven)
- Video ad script — product demo
- Video ad script — before/after transformation
- Video storyboard outline (scene-by-scene with visual + audio direction)
- Hook variations (first 3 seconds — multiple angles)
- Video brief for editor/creator (specs, pacing, b-roll notes, CTA overlay timing)

### Copy Frameworks
- Problem-agitate-solve ad copy
- Testimonial/social proof ad copy
- Listicle-style ad copy
- Newsjacking / trend-based ad copy
- Offer-led / promo ad copy
- Storytelling / narrative ad copy

### Reporting & Optimization
- Weekly performance report (KPIs, winners/losers, next actions)
- Monthly performance report (trend analysis, budget reallocation recs)
- Creative performance ranking (with win/loss reasoning)
- Optimization recommendations (bid strategy, placement, audience, creative swaps)
- Kill list (underperforming ads/audiences to pause with reasoning)

### Account Management
- Campaign naming convention guide
- UTM parameter structure
- Campaign launch checklist
- QA checklist (pixel, events, audience, placements, copy, links)

## 2. Google Ads Agent

### Strategy & Planning
- Campaign strategy brief (Search, Shopping, Display, YouTube, Performance Max, Demand Gen)
- Account structure recommendation (campaign/ad group architecture)
- Budget allocation plan (cross-campaign, cross-network)
- Bidding strategy recommendation (tCPA, tROAS, Max Conversions — with rationale)
- Testing framework (ad copy, landing page, bid strategy, audience tests)
- Competitor positioning analysis (auction insights breakdown)

### Research & Analysis
- Ad account audit (structure, Quality Score, wasted spend, search term leakage, negative gaps)
- Keyword research (grouped by theme, with volume, competition, CPC estimates)
- Search term analysis (converting terms, wasted spend terms, negative keyword opportunities)
- Competitor ad copy analysis (messaging patterns, offers, extensions — via Ads Transparency Center browser lookup or user-provided data)
- Quality Score analysis (expected CTR, ad relevance, landing page experience — per keyword)
- Impression share analysis (lost IS budget vs rank, with recommendations)
- Industry/vertical benchmark comparison

### Search Ads
- Responsive Search Ad copy (15 headlines + 4 descriptions, pinning recommendations)
- Ad copy variations by theme/angle (benefit-led, feature-led, offer-led, urgency-led)
- Dynamic keyword insertion templates
- Ad extension copy — sitelinks (headline + description x4-8)
- Ad extension copy — callouts
- Ad extension copy — structured snippets
- Ad extension copy — call, location, price, promotion extensions

### Shopping / Performance Max
- Product feed title optimization recommendations
- Product feed description optimization
- Performance Max asset group copy (headlines, long headlines, descriptions)
- Performance Max audience signal recommendations
- Shopping campaign structure (brand vs non-brand, priority settings)
- Merchant Center issue resolution guide

### Display Ads
- Display ad copy (responsive display — headlines, long headline, descriptions)
- Display targeting strategy (audiences, topics, placements, contextual)
- Remarketing audience strategy (lists, windows, bid adjustments)

### YouTube Ads
- YouTube ad script — skippable in-stream (hook in 5s, 15-30s total)
- YouTube ad script — non-skippable (15s)
- YouTube ad script — Shorts
- YouTube ad script — bumper (6s)
- YouTube targeting strategy (audiences, topics, placements, channels)
- YouTube creative brief for production team

### Demand Gen
- Demand Gen campaign asset copy (headlines, descriptions, CTAs)
- Demand Gen audience strategy
- Image/carousel creative brief for Demand Gen

### Reporting & Optimization
- Weekly performance report (Search, Shopping, PMax, Display, YouTube)
- Monthly performance report with trend analysis
- Search term report with negative keyword additions
- Optimization recommendations (bids, budgets, match types, ad rotation, audiences)
- Wasted spend analysis (with cut list)
- Conversion tracking audit (tag setup, attribution model review)

### Account Management
- Campaign naming convention guide
- Negative keyword list (campaign and shared library)
- UTM parameter structure
- Campaign launch checklist
- Conversion tracking setup guide

## 3. SEO Agent

### Strategy & Planning
- SEO strategy brief (priorities, quick wins, long-term plays)
- Content strategy / editorial calendar (keyword-mapped topics by month)
- Link building strategy (outreach targets, methods, anchor text plan)
- Technical SEO roadmap (prioritized issue list with impact estimates)
- Local SEO strategy (GMB, citations, local content plan)
- International SEO strategy (hreflang, subdomain/subfolder recommendations)
- Site migration SEO plan (redirect map, risk mitigation, monitoring plan)

### Research & Analysis
- Full site SEO audit (technical, on-page, content, backlink profile)
- Technical SEO audit (crawlability, indexation, Core Web Vitals, schema, mobile)
- On-page SEO audit (title tags, meta descriptions, headings, internal links, content gaps)
- Backlink profile analysis (quality distribution, toxic links, competitor gap)
- Keyword research (clustered by topic, intent, volume, difficulty, SERP features)
- Keyword gap analysis (vs competitors — missing opportunities)
- SERP analysis (feature breakdown, competitor content format, ranking signals)
- Content gap analysis (topics competitors rank for that you don't)
- Competitor SEO teardown (domain authority, top pages, content strategy, link sources)
- Page-level SEO analysis (single URL deep-dive with fix list)
- Cannibalization analysis (pages competing for same keywords)
- Internal linking audit

### On-Page Content
- SEO-optimized page title + meta description (with variants)
- Heading structure recommendation (H1-H4 hierarchy for a page)
- Content brief (target keyword, secondary keywords, intent, outline, word count, SERP notes)
- Blog post outline (SEO-optimized structure with headings, target keywords, internal link suggestions)
- Full blog post / article draft (SEO-optimized, for human review and editing)
- Product/service page copy (SEO-optimized)
- Category page copy
- FAQ section (keyword-targeted, schema-ready Q&A pairs)
- Image alt text recommendations

### Technical SEO
- Redirect map (301 redirect plan for migrations or URL changes)
- Robots.txt recommendations
- XML sitemap audit / recommendations
- Schema markup recommendations (with JSON-LD snippets)
- Core Web Vitals fix recommendations
- Crawl budget optimization recommendations
- Log file analysis (based on user-uploaded server logs)
- Canonical tag audit

### Link Building
- Link prospect list (relevant sites with domain metrics and outreach angle — via DataForSEO)
- Outreach email templates (guest post, resource link, broken link, HARO)
- Digital PR pitch ideas (data-driven story angles for earning links)
- Linkable asset brief (what to create to attract links — tools, studies, guides)

### Reporting
- Monthly SEO performance report (rankings, traffic, conversions, technical health)
- Quarterly SEO review (progress vs targets, strategy adjustments)
- Ranking tracker report (keyword position changes with analysis)

## 4. CRO Agent

### Strategy & Planning
- CRO strategy brief (priorities, hypothesis backlog, testing roadmap)
- Testing roadmap (prioritized A/B test queue — ICE or PIE scored)
- Experimentation framework (how to run tests, statistical significance, sample size)
- Personalization strategy (audience segments x page x messaging)

### Research & Analysis
- Full site CRO audit (page-by-page friction analysis, drop-off points, UX issues)
- Landing page teardown (above-fold, messaging, trust signals, CTA, form, mobile)
- Funnel analysis (step-by-step drop-off with hypotheses for each stage)
- Form analysis (field count, friction, error messaging, completion rate drivers)
- Checkout flow analysis (cart abandonment drivers with fix recommendations)
- Competitor UX/conversion teardown (what they do better, what to steal)
- User objection mapping (common objections by funnel stage + how to address)
- Trust signal audit (social proof, guarantees, credentials — gaps + recommendations)

### Testing
- A/B test hypothesis document (hypothesis, metric, expected impact, test design)
- A/B test brief (control vs variant description, dev specs, tracking requirements)
- A/B test results analysis (statistical significance, winner, learnings, next test)
- Multivariate test plan
- Test prioritization matrix (ICE or PIE scoring for backlog)

### Page-Level Optimization
- Landing page copy — full page (hero, benefits, social proof, CTA, FAQ)
- Landing page wireframe brief (section-by-section layout with content direction)
- Above-the-fold optimization recommendations (headline, subhead, CTA, hero image)
- Headline variations (benefit-driven, curiosity, social proof, urgency angles)
- CTA copy variations (button text + surrounding microcopy)
- Social proof section recommendations (testimonial format, placement, quantity)
- Pricing page optimization recommendations (anchoring, tier structure, feature comparison)
- Product page optimization (images, copy, reviews, urgency, cross-sells)
- Form optimization recommendations (fields, layout, microcopy, progressive disclosure)
- Exit-intent / popup strategy (trigger, copy, offer, design direction)
- Thank you / post-conversion page optimization

### Reporting
- Monthly CRO report (tests run, results, conversion rate trends, revenue impact)
- Test learnings library (cumulative log of what worked/didn't and why)
- Quarterly CRO review (program maturity, win rate, cumulative lift)

## 5. Email Agent

### Strategy & Planning
- Email marketing strategy brief (goals, segments, cadence, automation priorities)
- Email calendar / send schedule (campaigns mapped by week/month)
- List segmentation strategy (behavioral, demographic, engagement-based segments)
- Deliverability strategy (warm-up plan, authentication, list hygiene)
- Re-engagement / win-back strategy

### Research & Analysis
- Email program audit (deliverability, list health, engagement rates, automation gaps, design)
- Competitor email teardown (frequency, content types, design patterns, CTAs)
- Deliverability audit (analysis of user-provided DNS records, sender reputation data, and deliverability tool exports)
- List health analysis (bounces, unsubscribes, inactive subscribers, hygiene recommendations)
- Performance benchmarking (open rate, CTR, conversion vs industry benchmarks)

### Campaign Emails
- Promotional email copy (sale, launch, limited offer — subject line + preview text + body + CTA)
- Newsletter copy (curated content, updates, value-add)
- Product launch email (announcement, waitlist, early access)
- Event invitation email
- Survey / feedback request email
- Re-engagement email (win-back for cold subscribers)
- Seasonal / holiday campaign email
- Subject line variations (curiosity, benefit, urgency, personalization, question angles)
- Preview text variations
- A/B test plan for email (subject lines, send times, content, CTAs)

### Automated Sequences / Flows
- Welcome sequence (new subscriber — multi-email series)
- Onboarding sequence (new customer — multi-email series)
- Abandoned cart sequence (reminder, incentive, urgency — 3-5 emails)
- Browse abandonment sequence
- Post-purchase sequence (confirmation, tips, review request, cross-sell)
- Re-engagement / win-back sequence (graduated outreach for inactive subscribers)
- Lead nurture sequence (education-to-conversion drip)
- Upsell / cross-sell sequence
- Renewal / subscription reminder sequence
- Birthday / anniversary email
- Transactional email copy (order confirmation, shipping, password reset — optimized for brand)

### Email Design & Structure
- Email wireframe brief (layout, sections, content hierarchy)
- Email template recommendations (what templates to build for reuse)
- CTA optimization recommendations (button copy, placement, color)
- Mobile optimization recommendations

### Reporting
- Weekly email performance report (sends, opens, clicks, conversions, unsubscribes)
- Monthly email report (trends, top performers, segment performance, deliverability)
- Automation flow performance report (per-flow metrics with optimization recs)
- Quarterly email program review

## 6. Marketing Strategy Agent

### Strategy & Planning
- Full marketing strategy brief (goals, audience, channels, budget, KPIs, timeline)
- Go-to-market plan (new product/service launch — positioning, channels, timeline, messaging)
- Quarterly marketing plan (priorities, campaigns, budget allocation, KPIs)
- Annual marketing plan
- Channel mix recommendation (where to allocate budget across paid, organic, email, etc.)
- Budget allocation framework (by channel, by funnel stage, by objective)
- Marketing OKR / KPI framework (objectives, key results, leading/lagging indicators)
- Customer journey map (awareness to advocacy — touchpoints, messaging, channels)
- Funnel strategy (TOFU/MOFU/BOFU content + channel mapping)

### Research & Analysis
- Market research summary (TAM, trends, opportunities, threats)
- Competitive analysis (positioning, messaging, channels, strengths, weaknesses)
- Audience / buyer persona development (demographics, psychographics, pain points, motivations)
- SWOT analysis
- Brand positioning analysis (current vs desired positioning, competitive whitespace)
- Channel performance analysis (cross-channel attribution, efficiency, saturation)
- Marketing tech stack audit (tools, gaps, redundancies, integration issues)
- Customer acquisition cost analysis (CAC by channel, LTV:CAC ratio, payback period)

### Messaging & Positioning
- Brand messaging framework (value prop, positioning statement, tagline options, key messages)
- Value proposition development (per product/service/segment)
- Messaging hierarchy (primary, secondary, tertiary messages by audience)
- Elevator pitch / boilerplate copy (multiple lengths)
- Competitive differentiation messaging (why us vs them — by competitor)
- Audience-specific messaging variants (same product, different segments)

### Campaign Planning
- Campaign brief (objective, audience, channels, messaging, budget, timeline, KPIs)
- Campaign concept / big idea (creative theme, narrative, executional direction)
- Content marketing plan (formats, topics, distribution, repurposing strategy)
- Integrated campaign plan (how channels work together for a single initiative)
- Promotional calendar (offers, events, seasonal hooks mapped to calendar)

### Reporting & Analysis
- Monthly marketing dashboard (cross-channel KPIs, spend, conversions, ROI)
- Quarterly business review (performance vs targets, strategic adjustments)
- Campaign post-mortem (results, learnings, what to repeat/change)
- Marketing attribution analysis (which channels/touchpoints drive conversions)
- ROI analysis by channel

### Frameworks & Processes
- Marketing team workflow recommendations
- Agency brief template (for briefing external partners)
- Marketing SOP documentation
- Reporting cadence and format recommendations
